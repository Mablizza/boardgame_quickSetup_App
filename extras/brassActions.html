Brass Birmingham Actions

Complete reference guide for all player actions

How Actions Work

On your turn, perform a total of 2 actions. For each action, discard a card from your hand and place it face up on your discard pile. Click on any action below to see detailed instructions.

Build Action

Build Industry tiles on the board to expand your network and generate resources.

  1. Discard an appropriate card. Unlike other actions, the Build action requires an appropriate card be discarded. (Note: In 2/3-player games you may still build in any location.)
  2. Take the lowest level tile of the chosen industry from your Player Mat and place it (black top half face down) on an undeveloped space in your chosen build location, following these rules:
    • If possible, place it on a space displaying only that industry's icon
    • Otherwise, place it on a space displaying that industry's icon with another industry's icon
    • If no undeveloped spaces display your industry's icon, you may not place it in that location
  3. Pay the cost displayed on your Player Mat and consume any required iron and coal.
  4. If you built a Coal Mine or Iron Works: Place coal or iron on the tile, then move as many cubes as possible to the Market. For each cube moved, collect the corresponding money. If the last cube is moved, flip the tile and advance your Income Marker.
  5. If you built a Brewery: Place 1 beer barrel (Canal Era) or 2 beer barrels (Rail Era) on the tile.

Canal Era Building

  • Maximum 1 Industry tile per location
  • May share locations with other players
  • Cannot build tiles with black/white symbol

Rail Era Building

  • May build multiple Industry tiles per location
  • Cannot build tiles with blue/white symbol (use Develop Action)
Sell Action

Flip Cotton Mills, Manufacturers, and Pottery tiles by selling to Merchant tiles.

Requirements:

  • Connection to a Merchant tile
  • Beer (usually)
  1. Discard any card from your Hand
  2. Choose 1 unflipped Cotton Mill, Manufacturer, or Pottery tile connected to a Merchant tile with that industry's icon
  3. Consume the required beer shown in the top right corner of the Industry tile. You may use beer from the Merchant tile space for bonus money
  4. Flip the Industry tile and advance your Income Marker by the spaces shown
  5. You may repeat this process for each of your unflipped Industry tiles
Loan Action

Acquire money by taking loans. Loans are never repaid but lower your income level.

  1. Discard any card from your Hand
  2. Take £30 from the bank and move your Income Marker 3 income levels backwards. Place your marker on the highest space within the new lower income level
Scout Action

Acquire Wild cards for increased building flexibility.

Wild Industry Card

Can be played as any Industry card

Wild Location Card

Can be played as any Location card

  1. Discard any card from your Hand, plus 2 additional cards (3 total)
  2. Take 1 Wild Location and 1 Wild Industry card
Note: When discarded, Wild cards return to their Card Draw Area, not your Discard Pile.
Develop Action

Remove Industry tiles from your Player Mat to access higher-level tiles without building lower levels.

  1. Discard any card from your Hand
  2. Remove 1 or 2 Industry tiles from your Player Mat and return them to the box. Each tile must be the lowest level of its industry when removed
  3. Consume 1 iron for each tile removed
Important: Pottery tiles with a lightbulb icon may not be developed. These can only be removed through the Build action.
Network Action

Place Link tiles to expand your network and gain access to new locations.

  1. Discard any card from your Hand
  2. Place a Link tile on an undeveloped line adjacent to a location in your network

Canal Era Network

  • Cannot build rail Links
  • May build canal Links on undeveloped canal lines
  • Maximum 1 canal Link for £3

Rail Era Network

  • Cannot build canal Links
  • May build rail Links on undeveloped railroad lines
  • 1 rail Link for £5
  • Maximum 2 rail Links for £15 + 1 beer
  • Consume 1 coal for each rail Link built
